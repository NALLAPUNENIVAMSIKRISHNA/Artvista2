Works of Leonardo da vinci

  • Mona Lisa

    The Mona Lisa is a half-length portrait painting by Italian artist Leonardo da Vinci the most parodied work of art in the world".

  • The last supper

    The Last Supper is a mural painting by the Italian High Renaissance artist Leonardo da Vinci, dated to c. 1495–1498.

  • Vitruvian Man

    The Vitruvian Man depicts a nude man facing forward and surrounded by a square, while superimposed on a circle. The man is portrayed in different stances

  • Annunciation

    The Annunciation, also referred to as the Annunciation to the Blessed Virgin Mary, the Annunciation of Our Lady, or the Annunciation of the Lord, is the announcement by the archangel Gabriel to Mary

  • Virgin of the Rocks

    The Virgin with the Infant Saint John the Baptist Adoring the Christ Child Accompanied by an Angel or La Vierge, l'Enfant Jésus, saint Jean Baptiste et un ange, also called The Madonna of the Rocks
